SILKSONG IS COMING OUT 2025

Hollow Knight: Silksong is an upcoming Metroidvania-style video game developed by Team Cherry, the creators of the critically acclaimed Hollow Knight. Silksong is expected to serve as either a sequel or prequel to its predecessor and is rumored to be even bigger than it's pretacesor.

First announced in February 2019, updates on its development have been sparse, but Team Cherry continues to assure fans that the game remains in active production.

It has now been confirmed in the Nintendo Switch 2 Direct, held on 2.4.2025 that Silksong will be realeased some time in 2025! This is extremely exiting news for the community as most of them have been waiting for the game since the original 2019 announcement.

There has not yet been a specific release date, but fans are speculating that the release might happen some time in the summer because of an announcement made by Xbox.
